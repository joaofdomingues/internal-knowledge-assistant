SITE SAFETY PROCEDURES

All personnel entering the construction site must wear a hard hat,
high-visibility vest, and steel-toe boots at all times. Visitors must
sign in at the site office and be accompanied by an authorized
supervisor.

Scaffolding above 2 meters must be inspected weekly by a certified
technician and tagged with the inspection date. Any scaffolding
without a valid inspection tag must not be used.

In case of an accident, the site supervisor must be notified
immediately, and an incident report must be filed within 24 hours
using the standard company incident form.

Fire extinguishers must be located within 15 meters of any active
welding or cutting operation, and all welding personnel must complete
the annual hot-works safety training.
