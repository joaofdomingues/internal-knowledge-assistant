PROCUREMENT POLICY

Any purchase order below 1,000 EUR can be approved directly by a team
lead. Purchase orders between 1,000 EUR and 10,000 EUR require sign-off
from the department manager. Purchases above 10,000 EUR require
approval from the finance director and at least two competing
quotes from different vendors.

All vendors must be registered in the approved supplier list before
an order can be placed. New vendors are added to the list after a
compliance check by the procurement team, which typically takes 5
business days.

Invoices must reference a valid purchase order number. Invoices
without a PO number will be returned to the vendor and payment will
be delayed until the PO is confirmed.
