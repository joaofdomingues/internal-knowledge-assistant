NEW EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING GUIDE

New employees receive a company laptop and email account on their
first day. IT setup requests should be submitted at least 3 business
days before the employee's start date.

Employees must complete mandatory training modules within the first
two weeks: general safety induction, data protection (GDPR), and
company code of conduct.

Probation period is 90 days for all new hires. A performance review
meeting with the direct manager is scheduled at the 45-day and 90-day
marks.

Access badges for site visits must be requested through the facilities
team at least 48 hours in advance.
